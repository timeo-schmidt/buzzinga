Noise cancelling

Wearing noise-cancelling headphone or listening to loud music? Stay informed about important sounds around you.

Hearing disability

Giving people with hearing disabilities access to the world of sounds around them.

Door bell

Never overhear a doorbell again and get notified instantly if someone is at the door. No expensive hardware or installation required.

Fire alarm

Be notified about critical alerts such as fire-alarms around you or even if you are away from home.

Always listening, so you don't have to.

Our state-of-the art audio classification algorithms use advanced machine learning models to constantly classify sounds they pick up. All processing is done directly on the device and is never sent across the internet. Absolute privacy guaranteed!

>98%

Accuracy

Very high performing convolutional neural network for sound classification. Average F1 score on representative test set.

700mW

Power Consumption

Our device consumes does all the hard work and consumes as much power as your switched-off TV.

25k

Training Samples
